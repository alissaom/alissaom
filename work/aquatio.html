    PodSquad: Aquatio

    HackFSU 2016

    UI/UX and Web design - branding and graphics

    Overview

    HackFSU 2016 was a 30-hour hackathon gathering over 400 students from all over the state of Florida and the Southeast for a fun-filled weekend to create, design, and build.

    The Story

    After attending the iOS Workshop sponsored by Apple, our team decided to challenge ourselves and create an iOS game using Swift.

    We want to bring awareness to the damage we inflict on our oceans. Every year 5.25 trillion plastic debris pollutes our oceans while having a lifespan of up to 200 years. We hope to remind the masses to Reduce, Reuse and Recycle in order to save the ocean

    Learn more about Aquatio here!

    Check out the final product - Aquatio!

    My Role

    For this project, I worked on graphic design, game design elements, and the game's landing page using Photoshop, Illustrator, CSS, HTML, and JS.

    I also used Xcode to create and program our game's Start Screen with Swift.

    To create and brand our product, I sought help from a mentor at HackFSU. I was about to learn how to effectily communicate my vision with my potential users through an app landing page. This process included crafting user-friendly phrases, creating eye-catching graphics, and following design rules and fundamentals.
