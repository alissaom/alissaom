    About CGS

    Campus Girl Scouts (CGS) is a Florida State University student-run outreach organization geared towards helping the Florida Panhandle troops gains leadership, science, and teachnology skills for their respective badges.

    Since learning about the Girl Scouts organization in late high school, I've always dreamed of being a Girl Scout. When I found out about Campus Girl Scouts and their mission, I immediately jumped in. Who doesn't want to be paid in Girl Scout Cookies? Plus, you become an official Adult Girl Scout! I served as the Vice President for CGS for two years in college where I led and volunteered at different camps and workshops.
