    About Florida TSA

    The Florida Technology Student Association (TSA) is the state division of the national organization - Technology Student Association. Florida TSA focuses on building leadership and technical skills of students in middle through high school. Every year, the Florida chapters send their leadership team to Orlando for their annual Leadership Conference.

    The STARS (Students in Technology, Academia, Research, & Service) chapter at Florida State University helps lead the different teams and present on leadership, communication, and technology topics. I've had the pleasure to participate as a Group Leader and presenter for their 2015 and 2016 leadership conferences.
